Our Mission

To revolutionize agricultural supply chains by connecting farmers directly with retailers, ensuring fair prices, transparent transaction and sustainable relationships.

Our Story

BharatFarmiConnect by technology entrepreneurs who saw the inefficiencies and problem in traditional farm-to-market supply chains. We recognized that both farmers and retailers were losing out due to multiple middlemen and lack of transparency.

What began as a small pilot project in Maharashtra has now grown into a nationwide platform serving thousands of farmers and retailers across India. Our success is built on the real impact we've created - increased incomes for farmers and better quality produce for retailers.

Today, we're proud to be transforming agricultural commerce one direct connection at a time.Farmer-to-consumer direct supply chains offer benefits like fresher produce, potentially lower prices for consumers, and better prices for farmers by reducing reliance on intermediaries. This model also enables transparency and allows consumers to support local agriculture
