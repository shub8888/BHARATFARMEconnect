Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Register" button in the top navigation. You'll need to choose whether you're a farmer or retailer, then fill in your basic information. After verifying your email, your account will be active.

What are the fees for using FarmConnect?

FarmConnect charges a small transaction fee of 2% for successful sales. There are no upfront costs or subscription fees. Our pricing model ensures we only succeed when you succeed.

How do I resolve a dispute with a buyer/seller?

First, try to resolve the issue directly with the other party through our messaging system. If you can't reach an agreement, you can file a dispute through our resolution center. Our support team will review the case and help find a fair solution.
